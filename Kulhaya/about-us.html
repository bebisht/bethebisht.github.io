ABOUT US

Leading website of Purnia Division

Population of Purnia division more than one crore.

The history of kulhaiya Awaaz begins with the Facebook. The domain was booked on 25th-Jun-2015, Kulhaiya.com started officially since 7 July 2016. It is the first kulhaiya community based website for the people of Purnia, Katihar, Kishanganj and Araria district of Bihar. Kulhaiya.com is an online platform of 3.5 million (kulhaiya population 3.5 millions) along with one crore people of Purnea, Araria, Kishanganj, Katihar and Morang (Nepal) to share truthful information, idea, experience & knowledge like a family member.
